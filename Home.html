Smart City Road Solution

Road traffic refers to the movement of vehicles and pedestrians on
roads and highways. It is essential for transportation but can lead to congestion, accidents, and pollution if not managed properly. Common causes of traffic issues include high vehicle numbers, poor road conditions, and lack of proper traffic control. Effective solutions such as improved public transport, better road infrastructure, and strict traffic laws can help reduce congestion and ensure safer travel for everyone.
