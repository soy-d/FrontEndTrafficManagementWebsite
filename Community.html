Raising Awareness on Road Traffic Safety in Ja-Ela.

As part of a broader effort to improve public safety and transportation efficiency, a social awareness session was conducted in Ja-Ela focusing on the growing issue of road traffic and congestion. This session aimed to educate the local community—including pedestrians, motorists, and students—about responsible road behavior, traffic rule adherence, and the importance of cooperation between road users. Emphasis was placed on understanding how minor violations and lack of awareness contribute to large-scale traffic problems, especially during peak hours. The session also introduced practical solutions, such as using alternative routes, public transport, and real-time traffic monitoring tools like Google Maps. Through open discussions and presentations, the session encouraged participants to become active contributors to safer and more organized road environments in the Ja-Ela area.
