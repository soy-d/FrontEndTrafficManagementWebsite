Solution for the Road Traffic

Using Non-Functional Traffic Lights as a Temporary Traffic Solution

Click the picture to explore how colorful lights function.

Utilizing traffic light colors that are currently non-functional can serve as an innovative temporary solution to manage road traffic. By repurposing these lights through manual signaling or alternative power sources, authorities can guide drivers safely at intersections, reduce confusion, and improve traffic flow. This approach helps maintain order until full repair or restoration is completed.

Using Google Maps and Alternative Routes to Avoid Traffic Congestion

Tap the image to discover the smoothest, traffic-free route.

During heavy traffic or road congestion, it's advisable to use alternative routes to avoid delays. Drivers should rely on navigation tools like Google Maps to check real-time traffic conditions and choose the fastest, least crowded path. This proactive approach helps reduce overall traffic pressure, saves time, and ensures smoother travel, especially during peak hours or unexpected roadblocks.
